## Tips
- K-Means is sensitive to initialization. Always set random_state=42 and n_init=10 for reproducibility.
- The Elbow Method does not always give you a clear "elbow." If ambiguous, pick fewer clusters and justify it.
- Look at cluster centers (km.cluster_centers_) — they tell you the average feature values for each group.
- Business recommendations should be actionable. "Do more marketing" is not actionable.
- In Uzbekistan, Telegram is the dominant messaging platform. If suggesting customer outreach, Telegram and SMS are more realistic than email.
